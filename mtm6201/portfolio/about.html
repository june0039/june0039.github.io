Love With Passion

Love with Passion began as a labour
of love for artist Julie Juneau. Julie
went back to college in her later years
after nursing for 12 years and raising
three boys on her own. Realizing that there was no free time to work on her
passions for art, she decided to make it her work instead! During her first year
of college of Interactive Media Design, she found her passion in photography and Web Design.
Julie began focusing on independent freelance work while
finishing up school. Love with Passion began as an online
freelance page created in UX/UI Design classes and completed in Web Design
Julie is now photographing weddings, designing logos and
completing her course for a diploma.
Plans for a studio and gardens are in the near future!

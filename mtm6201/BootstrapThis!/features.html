Cards

Cards are a very useful tool, built into bootstrap for anyones use. You can just copy the code and paste it into your program in Visio Studio, like all their features, for a more professional and visual look. Each card has space for am image plus a small description. At the Bottom, you can add other comments and or links as well.

Carousel

Here is an example of bootstraps components carousel. It is used to dsiplay a carousel of multiple images uploaded into the code. It will basicaly, keep moving through a carousel of the images you upload into the carousel code over and over. Like a mini slideshow of your images. You can choose different variations from getBootstrap.com such as indicators, arrows showing the direction to flip images. Captions, which allo you to also write a caption on your images. They even have a cross fade code to add a fade out on the first image.
